How to play Lil Slasher Shoot

Copy the entire Lil Slasher Shoot folder to your computer
Launch lil_slasher_shoot.exe

Click the Play button or press 'p' to begin
Left and Right arrow keys to move
Spacebar to shoot
Press 'q' to quit

Your high score will save after death and remain even after closing the game

This game is based on a tutorial from No Starch Press' Book, Python Crash Course
Author: Eric Matthes
